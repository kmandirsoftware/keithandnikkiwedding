Traveling to Our Wedding

We understand that many of our guests will be traveling from out of state and we hope to provide you with all of the necessary information to make your travel to Maryland as easy as possible.

DIA

The best airport to fly into is Denver International (DIA). The drive from DIA to Cherry Creek is a little less than half an hour (30 miles).

Light Rail from DIA to Cherry Creek

For those of you who are sharing cars, but who may not be arriving at the airport at the same time, there's the DIA Light Rail.

The new Denver airport rail has six stops between the airport and Denver Union Station. The ride takes about 37 minutes. The rates are $9 for one person each way.
